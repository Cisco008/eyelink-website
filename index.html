🥗 Eye Health & Wellness

The Danger of Hidden Sugars

High sugar intake can damage the tiny blood vessels in your retina. Cutting back on processed sweets helps prevent vision blurriness and long-term eye disease like diabetic retinopathy.

Eat Your Greens

Swap the candy for Spinach and Kale! These are packed with Lutein and Zeaxanthin—nutrients that act as a "natural sunblock" for your eyes.

Banting & Retinal Health

Low-carb lifestyles like Banting and Carnivore focus on stabilizing blood sugar. By reducing insulin spikes, you help protect the delicate blood vessels in your eyes from inflammation and leaking.
